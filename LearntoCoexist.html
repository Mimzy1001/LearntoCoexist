Home

Learn to Coexist is a website consisting of different stories, folktales, and myths from various parts of the world. The purpose is to compare and contrast the stories from many cultures and realize how each individual story is able to shape those cultures and how they can all be important in different ways. We have provided a variety of stories with different genres in order to allow for easy and effective comparison between cultures.

About Us

We are part of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in Chicago where we learned about computer science and coding. The five of us decided to come together and combine our ideas in order to create this website to bring awareness to the cultures around us.
